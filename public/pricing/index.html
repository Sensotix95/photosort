No. All updates are included in the one-time purchase. When PhotoSort's AI model improves or new features are added, you get them automatically. There is no version 2.0 that requires a new purchase.

Yes. PhotoSort runs in the browser, so you can use it on any computer with Chrome or Edge. Your purchase is tied to your payment rather than a specific device. If you need to use PhotoSort on a second computer, simply sign in when prompted — or contact us for details on multi-device access.
